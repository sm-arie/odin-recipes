Gnocchi Tofu Bolognese

Description

A delicious creamy vegan bolognese sauce with tofu and potato gnocchi. Serves 2.

Ingredients

  • 400g vegan gnocchi
  • 200g tofu
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 100ml coconut milk
  • 100ml hot vegetable stock
  • 50g vegan cheese, grated
  • 2 carrots
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 onion
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano, basil, rosemary, thyme
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Press or boil the tofu, mash it or cut it into cubes to scramble while frying. Peel and cut the onion, garlic and carrot.
  2. Heat the oil in a frying pan. Add tofu, carrot and onion, then garlic, in that order. Fry, stirring occasionally until it looks nice.
  3. Add tomatoes, coconut milk, vegetable stock and herbs. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add the gnocchi to the pan. Let it simmer for about 5 more minutes. Make sure it's not too soupy or dry, and season to taste.
  5. Transfer everything from the pan to an oven-proof dish and top with vegan cheese. Cook at 180 degrees for 10-15 minutes.
