Mystery Sweet

Description

A recipe I found screenshotted on my phone with no title.

I've never made it, so don't know what it is, but it's vegan!

Ingredients

  • 230g smooth peanut butter
  • 200ml maple syrup
  • 100g coconut flour
  • 100g dark chocolate
  • 2tbsp coconut oil
  • Flaky salt

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine the peanut butter and maple syrup.
  2. Add the coconut flour. Mix until fully combined.
  3. Transfer the batter into a small dish lined with baking paper. Refrigerate for 10 minutes.
  4. Melt the dark chocolate and coconut oil together.
  5. Pour melted chocolate on top of the peanut butter layer and spread evenly.
  6. Refrigerate until hardened (20-30 mins).
  7. Cut into slices and sprinkle flaky salt on top. Store in the fridge.
